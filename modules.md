I hope this email finds you well. Following the completion of the proposal review phase, we are now moving to the next milestone: the Literature Review Report.

DEADLINE
Wednesday, April 01, 2026 (11:55 PM)

SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

- Submit BOTH .tex and .pdf files
- Use the provided template: Literature_Review_Template.tex
- Review a minimum of 8-12 relevant papers
- Papers should be from top-tier venues (KDD, ICDM, CIKM, NeurIPS, ICML, VLDB, SIGMOD, WWW, AAAI, etc.) published between 2020-2025

WHAT TO INCLUDE
Your literature review should cover:

- Introduction to your research area and problem
- Background concepts and terminology
- Organized survey of relevant papers (grouped by themes/approaches)
- Comparative analysis table of reviewed approaches
- Identification of research gaps
- Connection to your project objectives and approach

IMPORTANT REMINDERS

- Ensure equal contribution from all group members
- Use proper academic citations
- Focus on papers directly relevant to your project
- Critically analyze the literature, don't just summarize
